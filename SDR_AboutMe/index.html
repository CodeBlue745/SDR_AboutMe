My Intrigues

World of Warcraft is a video game with excellent 3-D graphics. Having played several hours of the game this past month, I enjoy the storyline, characters, quests, and collectable in-game items. Every now and then, my wife will join me because the game is multiplayer, with hundreds of online players from around the world. Each character is customizeable. There are several types of game characters, including mages, elves, humans and dwarves. However, the game loads much faster than a comparable Lord of the Rings multiplayer game. This is because the game uses only the essential scenic items to prevent computer lag.

Having always been interested in game development, I found a new tool to develop with on my Oculus device. Few people even have the Oculus device or know what it is. An Oculus is a Virtual Reality headset that totally encapsulates your eyes. It's like the Google glasses, but with a container around the glasses. Some cool things that can be done with this device are: watching how-to videos, playing 360-games, and using Google Earth to explore incredible tourist attractions without leaving the comfort of your own home. Although, the device is not accepted by many people because of the danger it poses, there are many benefits to having a computer that presents a 3-D immersive reality as opposed to a 3-D reality on a traditional computer screen.

Reading is a great pasttime. Because I spend a lot of time developing pages and posts for websites, I have a goal to spend more time reading. I enjoy reading religious texts. Right now, I am reading from the New Testament. By coincidence, my entire ward is reading from the New Testament as well. So far, I have read from The Book of Mormon several times, The Old Testament (cover to cover and Numbers), and The Pearl of Great Price. I would like to read Doctrine and Covenants all the way through, too.
